Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Cosmo Catch respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is stored or processed when you use the app.

2. Information Stored Locally

The app may store the following data on your device:

  • Star Coin balance.
  • Achievement and run progress.
  • Transaction history shown inside the app.
  • Language, sound, vibration, and player name preferences.

This local gameplay data is used to provide the app experience.

3. Purchases

Optional Star Coin purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your card details or Google Play password. Purchase receipts may be logged for support, fraud prevention, and order reconciliation.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Precise location.
  • Contacts.
  • Photos, videos, or files.
  • Browsing history.
  • Advertising ID for ad targeting.
