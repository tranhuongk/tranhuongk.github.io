About Prism Pop

Prism Pop is a bright, satisfying tap-to-blast match puzzle by Galax Team. Tap groups of matching tiles to pop them, chase big combos, and turn coins into boosters and color themes.

Features

  • Two modes: Classic (target + limited moves) and Zen (endless).
  • Five boosters: Bomb, Color Blast, Hammer, Shuffle, +5 Moves.
  • Five color themes: Aurora, Sunset, Ocean, Candy, Neon.
  • Daily bonus, player profile, stats, and transaction history.
  • Six languages: English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, Russian.
  • Optional coin packs via Google Play. No ads.
