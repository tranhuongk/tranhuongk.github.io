About Galax Arcade

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Galax Arcade is a handcrafted retro collection that bundles three original 8-bit mini-games into a single Android app, sharing one Credits wallet across all of them.

Included Games

  • Gem Catch — drag Pip's basket to catch falling gems and dodge bombs.
  • Deep Dive — hold to swim up, release to sink. Collect pearls, dodge sharks and mines.
  • Plasma Run — side-scrolling run-and-gun on five neon battlefields.

Features

  • Original 8-bit pixel art (no third-party or AI-generated assets).
  • Single shared Credits wallet across all three games.
  • Five Plasma Run maps with rising difficulty.
  • Six languages: English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, Russian.
  • 16 in-app credit tiers from $0.49 to $99.99.
  • No ads, no analytics, no accounts — privacy-first.
