Privacy Policy for Galax Arcade

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Galax Arcade ("we", "our", or "the app") is an offline single-player pixel arcade collection bundling three mini-games (Gem Catch, Deep Dive, Plasma Run). This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. By using Galax Arcade you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

Galax Arcade does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. We do not run our own backend.

  • Game data (your credit balance, best score, chosen language and purchase history) is stored only on your device using local storage.
  • We do not request your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers.

In-App Purchases

Galax Arcade offers optional consumable credit packs purchased through Google Play Billing. Payments are processed entirely by Google. We never see or store your card details. Purchase processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

App Updates

The app uses the Google Play In-App Updates API to let you know when a newer version is available. This feature communicates only with Google Play and does not share personal data with us.

Children's Privacy

Galax Arcade features cartoonish pixel-art action with no blood and no gore. Because the app collects no personal data at all, it is suitable for general audiences.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Google Play (for billing and updates). No advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs are included.

Data Deletion

Because all data lives on your device, you can remove it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling Galax Arcade.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
