✦ ABOUT THE GAME

Star Path is an original puzzle game where you gaze into the night sky and connect stars to form beautiful constellations. Each level challenges you to discover the correct star sequence, revealing ancient patterns written across the cosmos.

✦ HOW TO PLAY

Look at the stars scattered across the night sky. Each constellation has a correct order — tap the stars in the right sequence to connect them with glowing lines. Complete the pattern to unlock the constellation's name and earn coins!
