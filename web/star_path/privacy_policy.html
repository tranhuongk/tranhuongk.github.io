⭐ PRIVACY POLICY

Star Path · GalaxTeam · Last updated: January 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

GalaxTeam ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Star Path collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Star Path collects minimal data:

  • Game progress data — levels completed, coins earned, hints used (stored locally on your device via SharedPreferences).
  • Purchase receipts — when you make an in-app purchase, Google Play processes the transaction. We store a local record of your purchase (product ID, coins awarded, timestamp) on your device only.
  • No personal data — we do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To save and restore your game progress.
  • To display your transaction history within the app.
  • To deliver purchased coins to your account.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties.

4. IN-APP PURCHASES

Star Path uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We never see or store your payment credentials. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing — for processing in-app purchases.

No analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries are included in Star Path.

6. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your data is as secure as your device. Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Star Path is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we collect no personal data at all, there are no special risks for children using this app.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. You may contact us to ask questions about our privacy practices.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
