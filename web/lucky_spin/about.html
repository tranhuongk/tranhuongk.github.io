About Cosmo Catch

Game Overview

Cosmo Catch is a quick five-lane arcade game. Move your rocket across the catch row, collect stars and crystals, dodge comets, and build combos through faster waves.

Every run starts free. Star Coins are optional and can be used for boosts such as shield and slow time.

Features

  • Five-lane catch-and-dodge arcade gameplay.
  • Escalating waves, lives, combos, and score chasing.
  • Optional Star Coin boosts for shield and slow time.
  • Daily bonus, achievements, and transaction history.
  • English and Vietnamese UI.
  • Secure optional Google Play Billing for consumable Star Coin packs.
