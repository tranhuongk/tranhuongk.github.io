Privacy Policy for Belt Bites

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Belt Bites is a single-player sushi sorting puzzle. This policy describes the information used by the app and its service providers.

Information and Game Data

Game progress, coin balance, settings, purchase history display, and an optional player name are stored locally on your device. No account is required.

To measure first opens and app reliability, the app may send an anonymous install identifier, event time, package name, app version, platform, language, and time zone to the developer's service. It does not send contacts, photos, messages, precise location, or health information.

Advertising

Belt Bites uses Google Mobile Ads for optional rewarded ads and occasional interstitial ads. Google and its partners may process device and advertising identifiers to deliver, limit, measure, and protect ads. You can manage advertising and privacy choices through your device and Google settings.

Purchases

Optional purchases are processed by Google Play. The app may record product ID, transaction reference, price, and an optional player name for fulfillment and support. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Service Providers

The app relies on Google Play Services, Google Play Billing, Google Mobile Ads, and a Supabase-hosted developer service. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy terms.

Children's Privacy

Belt Bites is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly request personal information from children. A parent or guardian should supervise purchases and advertising choices.

Retention and Deletion

You can remove local game data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app. To ask about deletion of a server-side diagnostic or purchase-support record, email us with the information needed to locate it.

Changes and Contact

We may update this policy when the app or its providers change. The current version will remain posted here with a revised date.
