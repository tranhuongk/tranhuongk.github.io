About Belt Bites

Belt Bites is a fast, satisfying sushi sorting puzzle. Tap plates from a winding conveyor, group three matching dishes, complete every order, and keep the kitchen from overflowing.

What is in the game?

Explore 40 levels across three chapters, relax in Zen mode, or take on a changing Daily Rush. Covered mystery plates, golden VIP dishes, combos, and optional boosters keep each service fresh.
