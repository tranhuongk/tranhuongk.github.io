Privacy Policy for Tidal Garden

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Overview

Tidal Garden stores most gameplay progress locally on your device. We only use limited information required for game functionality, in-app purchase support, and optional purchase logging.

2. Information We Use

  • Local game progress such as reef state, commissions, wallet values, and settings
  • Transaction history for successful in-app purchases
  • Optional player name used inside the app and for purchase log records
  • Best-effort imported player name from Treasure Quest on supported Android devices

3. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not store your payment card or billing credentials. Successful purchases may be logged to a Google Sheets endpoint for basic accounting or audit purposes.

4. Cross-App Profile Import

On Android, Tidal Garden may attempt to read a player name exported by Treasure Quest to prefill your profile the first time you launch the app. This is a one-time convenience import, not a live sync.

5. Data Storage

Core gameplay data is stored locally on your device. If remote purchase logging is configured, only limited transaction metadata is sent.

6. Your Choices

You may edit your player name, clear app data from your device settings, or stop using the app at any time.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact huongtv.uet@gmail.com.
