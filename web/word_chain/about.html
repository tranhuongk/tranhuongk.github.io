About Word Chain

Word Chain is an engaging word game that challenges players to connect words and form meaningful compound words in Vietnamese.

How to Play

Starting with one word from a compound word, you and the computer take turns adding another word to create a new meaningful compound word. The game continues until one player cannot form a valid word.
