About Word Chain

Word Chain is an engaging word game that challenges you to connect words and form meaningful compound words in Vietnamese. Take turns with an intelligent computer opponent and see who can form the longest chain!

How to Play

Starting with one word from a compound word, you and the computer alternate turns. Each player must add another word to create a new meaningful compound word. The game continues until one player cannot form a valid word.
