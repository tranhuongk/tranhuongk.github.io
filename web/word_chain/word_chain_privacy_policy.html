Privacy Policy for Word Chain

Last Updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Word Chain ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Game progress and scores
  • In-app purchase transactions

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent in app)
  • Game statistics and progress

1.3 In-App Purchases

When you make in-app purchases, we process transactions through Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Payment processing is handled entirely by the respective app store.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Process and manage in-app purchases
  • Track game progress and scores
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you updates about the App (if you opt-in)

3. Data Storage

Your game data and preferences are stored locally on your device. We may use cloud storage services to backup your game progress, but this data is encrypted and only accessible by you.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchase: For processing in-app purchases
  • Google Play Services / Apple Game Center: For game achievements and leaderboards (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of certain data collection practices
  • Request a copy of your data

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
