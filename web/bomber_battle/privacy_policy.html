Bomber Battle Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Bomber Battle is a mobile arcade game by Galax VN Team. Contact: huongtv.uet@gmail.com.

Information Stored Locally

The app may store gameplay progress, credit balance, best score, local transaction history, and an optional player name on your device. This data is used to provide the game experience.

Purchases

Optional Battle Credit purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your payment card details or Google Play password.

When a purchase is completed, the app may send limited purchase records to our backend tools for support, fraud prevention, and order reconciliation. These records can include the optional player name, app name, package name, product ID, purchase amount, currency, transaction or order ID, purchase token, platform, and event time.

Service Providers

We may use Google Play Billing, Google Sheets/Apps Script, and Supabase to process purchases, record purchase logs, and provide customer support. These service providers process data only for the purposes described in this policy.

Data Security and Retention

Purchase support records are transmitted over HTTPS and retained only as long as reasonably needed for support, accounting, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Your Choices

You can choose not to enter a player name. To request deletion or correction of purchase support records, contact us at huongtv.uet@gmail.com with your app name and order details.

Data We Do Not Collect

Bomber Battle does not collect precise location, contacts, photos, videos, files, browsing history, or advertising ID for ad targeting.

Children

Bomber Battle is not specifically designed for children. Parents and guardians should review device purchase controls before allowing purchases.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
