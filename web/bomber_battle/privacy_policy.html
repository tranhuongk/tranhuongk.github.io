Bomber Battle Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Bomber Battle is a mobile arcade game by Galax VN Team. Contact: huongtv.uet@gmail.com.

Information Stored Locally

The app may store gameplay progress, credit balance, best score, local transaction history, and an optional player name on your device. This data is used to provide the game experience.

Purchases

Optional Battle Credit purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your payment card details or Google Play password. Purchase receipts may be logged for support, fraud prevention, and order reconciliation.

Data We Do Not Collect

Bomber Battle does not collect precise location, contacts, photos, videos, files, browsing history, or advertising ID for ad targeting.

Children

Bomber Battle is not specifically designed for children. Parents and guardians should review device purchase controls before allowing purchases.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
