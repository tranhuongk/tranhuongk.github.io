Privacy Policy for Brain Gems

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Brain Gems. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Brain Gems is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Game Progress: We store your quiz scores, gem balance, and statistics locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, transaction details are processed through Google Play Store. We only receive confirmation of successful transactions.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Process your in-app purchases
  • Save your progress and statistics
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

4. Data Storage

All your game data (progress, gems, statistics) is stored locally on your device. We do not store this data on external servers. If you uninstall the app, this data will be deleted.

5. In-App Purchases

Brain Gems offers in-app purchases for gem packages. All transactions are processed securely through Google Play Store's payment system. We do not collect or store your payment information.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service.

7. Children's Privacy

Brain Gems is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require personal information to play.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
