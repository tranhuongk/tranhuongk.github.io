About Deep Diver

The Game

Deep Diver is an original 8-bit one-touch diving arcade. Hold the screen to swim up and release to sink as you guide your diver ever deeper. Scoop up glowing pearls and treasure chests, dodge the sharks and drifting sea mines, and see how deep you can go. The deeper your dive, the bigger your haul of pearls.

Features

  • Hand-crafted, fully original 8-bit pixel art
  • Addictive one-finger "hold to rise, release to sink" controls
  • Six languages: English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, Russian
  • Optional in-app pearl packs
  • Family-friendly, no ads, offline play
