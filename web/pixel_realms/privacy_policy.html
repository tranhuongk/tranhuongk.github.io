Privacy Policy — Pixel Realms

Last updated: 2026-05-24

Pixel Realms ("the App") is published by GalaxTeam. This page explains what the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

The App is designed to work without an account. We do not collect personal information directly. The following data is processed locally on your device:

  • Game progress — coins, unlocked realms, completed realms, best tap counts. Stored in SharedPreferences on-device.
  • Language preference — the selected UI language.
  • Purchase history — local record of in-app purchases for the Journal screen. The transaction itself is handled by Google Play Billing.

2. In-app purchases

Purchases of Pixel Coin packs are processed by Google Play Billing. Pixel Realms does not see or store your payment details. Receipts are handled by Google; refunds are managed through your Google Play account.

3. Google Play services

The App uses Google Play services for billing and (Android only) Google Play In-App Updates. Their data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Children

Pixel Realms is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data through Google Play, please contact us so we can remove it.

5. Permissions

The App requests only the INTERNET permission, used by the in-app billing client and the in-app updates checker. No location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage permissions are requested.

6. Data retention & deletion

All local data lives only on your device. Uninstalling the App removes it. You can also clear App data from Android Settings → Apps → Pixel Realms → Storage.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Significant changes will be highlighted inside the App.
