About Pixel Realms

An explorer's atlas, drawn in 8 bits.

Pixel Realms is a lightweight, original pixel-art exploration game. Each level is a "realm" — a small fog-of-war grid where you tap tiles to reveal hidden relics. Find every relic in a realm to mark it surveyed in your Field Journal.

What's inside

  • 60 hand-tuned realms across 20 distinct biomes.
  • Compass-guided HUD with a parchment Atlas.
  • Field Journal for survey history and purchases.
  • Six languages: English, Tiếng Việt, 中文, ไทย, हिन्दी, Русский.
  • Pixel Coin shop via Google Play Billing.
