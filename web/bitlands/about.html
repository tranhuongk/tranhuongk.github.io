About Bitlands

The Game

Bitlands is an original 8-bit pixel arcade. Guide Pip the pixel sprite and his basket to catch glowing gems falling through the neon Bitlands sky, while dodging the bombs. Every point you score becomes gems you keep.

Features

  • Hand-crafted, fully original 8-bit pixel art
  • Fast one-finger "Gem Catch" arcade gameplay
  • Six languages: English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, Russian
  • Optional in-app gem packs
  • Family-friendly, no ads, offline play
