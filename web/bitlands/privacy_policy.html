Privacy Policy for Bitlands

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Bitlands ("we", "our", or "the app") is an offline single-player pixel arcade game. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. By using Bitlands you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

Bitlands does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. We do not run our own backend.

  • Game data (your gems balance, best score, chosen language and purchase history) is stored only on your device using local storage.
  • We do not request your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers.

In-App Purchases

Bitlands offers optional consumable gem packs purchased through Google Play Billing. Payments are processed entirely by Google. We never see or store your card details. Purchase processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

App Updates

The app uses the Google Play In-App Updates API to let you know when a newer version is available. This feature communicates only with Google Play and does not share personal data with us.

Children's Privacy

Bitlands is family-friendly and does not knowingly collect any data from children. Because the app collects no personal data at all, it is suitable for all ages.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Google Play (for billing and updates). No advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs are included.

Data Deletion

Because all data lives on your device, you can remove it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling Bitlands.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
