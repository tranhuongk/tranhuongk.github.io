About Plasma Troopers

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Plasma Troopers is a one-thumb retro run-and-gun arcade with original pixel art and split-touch controls. Run, jump, and blast rogue drones across neon-lit ruins, dodge enemy fire, grab energy chips, and survive boss waves every 25 seconds.

Features

  • Original 8-bit pixel art (no third-party or AI-generated assets).
  • Split-touch controls — tap left to jump, tap right to shoot.
  • Three enemy classes — drones, mechs, and twin-cannon bosses.
  • Power-ups: energy chips for credits, hearts to restore health.
  • Six languages: English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, Russian.
  • 16 in-app credit tiers from $0.49 to $99.99.
  • No ads, no analytics, no accounts — privacy-first.
