Last updated: December 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Viet Riddles ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Game Progress: We store your game progress, scores, and solved riddles locally on your device.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type and operating system version for app functionality.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app to improve user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Save your game progress and preferences
  • Process in-app purchases

  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

All your game data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers except for:

  • In-app purchase processing through Google Play Store
  • Loading web content for Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and other informational pages

5. In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchases for coins that can be used within the game. All payment processing is handled by Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information such as credit card details.

Purchase records are stored locally on your device for transaction history purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of data collection by not using the app

9. Data Retention

Your game data is retained on your device until you:
