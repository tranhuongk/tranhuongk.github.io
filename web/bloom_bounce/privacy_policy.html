Bloom Bounce Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

Bloom Bounce is a kid-friendly game by Galax Team. Contact: huongtv.uet@gmail.com.

Information We Store

The app stores gameplay progress, coin balance, local transaction history, language preference, Pro entitlement, and an optional player name on the device. The player name can be entered manually or prefilled from Treasure Quest when available on the same Android device.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. After a successful purchase, Bloom Bounce may send a purchase log containing player name, app name, product id, amount, timestamp, week, and year to our purchase log endpoint for reconciliation. Purchase fulfillment does not depend on this log succeeding.

Advertising

Bloom Bounce may show ads using Google Mobile Ads. Ad requests are configured for child-directed treatment and a maximum ad content rating of G. Bloom Pro removes ads while the subscription is active.

Children

The app is designed to avoid chat, social sharing, precise location, gambling, violent content, and unnecessary personal data collection. Parents or guardians should review purchases and device-level parental controls.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
