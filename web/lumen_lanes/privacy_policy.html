Lumen Lanes Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Lumen Lanes is an original lane-shifting light runner by Galax Team. Contact: huongtv.uet@gmail.com.

Information Stored on Device

The app stores local game data such as coin balance, booster inventory, selected skin, stats, language preference, purchase history, and an optional player name. The player name can be typed manually or prefilled from Treasure Quest when that app is installed on the same Android device and the platform allows access.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. After a successful purchase, Lumen Lanes may send a purchase log containing player name, app name, product id, amount, timestamp, week, and year to our purchase log endpoint for reconciliation. Purchase fulfillment does not depend on this log succeeding.

Advertising

Lumen Lanes does not contain ads and does not use advertising SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. We do not collect precise location, contacts, health data, or device identifiers for tracking.

Children

The app avoids chat, social sharing, precise location, gambling, and user-generated content. Parents or guardians should review Google Play purchase controls.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
