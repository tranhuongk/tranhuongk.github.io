About Lumen Lanes

Lumen Lanes is an original five-lane light runner by Galax Team. Shift lanes, collect lumen orbs, cross energy gates, avoid red barriers, and use optional boosters to extend each run.

Features

  • Rush Run and Focus Drift modes.
  • Five boosters: Shield, Slow Time, Phase Blink, Magnet, and Pulse.
  • Unlockable runner skins, profile stats, daily bonus, and transaction history.
  • Six languages: English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, and Russian.
  • Optional consumable coin packs through Google Play. No ads.
