Privacy Policy for Block Glow

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Block Glow is an offline single-player block puzzle game. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Block Glow does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to our servers. The app does not request your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers.

Local Game Data

Game progress, best score, selected language, sound setting, and unlocked adventure progress are stored locally on your device using app storage. This data stays on your device.

Ads and Purchases

Block Glow does not include advertising SDKs and does not offer in-app purchases.

App Updates

The app may use Google Play In-App Updates to check whether a newer version is available. This feature communicates with Google Play and does not share personal data with us.

Children's Privacy

Block Glow is designed as a general-audience puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

You can delete local game data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app from your device.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
