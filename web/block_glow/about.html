About Block Glow

Block Glow is a relaxing neon block puzzle game with classic score chasing and handcrafted adventure levels.

Highlights

  • Drag and drop glowing pieces on an 8x8 board.
  • Clear rows and columns to build combos.
  • Play offline with no timer, no ads, and no in-app purchases.
  • Switch between Classic mode and Adventure levels.
