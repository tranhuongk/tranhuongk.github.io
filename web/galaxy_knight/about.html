About Galaxy Knight

Galaxy Knight is a 2D action RPG dungeon adventure by Galax Team. Slay monsters, unlock heroes, upgrade your knight, and rescue what the darkness has taken.

Features

  • 6+ levels with rising difficulty.
  • 50+ unlockable heroes and permanent upgrades.
  • Consumable potions, energy drinks, and fire scrolls.
  • Six languages: English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Thai, Hindi, Russian.
  • Optional coin packs via Google Play. No ads.

Credits

Built with the open-source Flame and Bonfire engines, based on Darkness Dungeon (MIT license).
