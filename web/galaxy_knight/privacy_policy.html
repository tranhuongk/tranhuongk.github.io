Galaxy Knight Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Galaxy Knight is a 2D action RPG by Galax Team. Contact: huongtv.uet@gmail.com.

Information We Store

The app stores gameplay progress, coin balance, owned heroes and upgrades, local transaction history, language preference, and an optional player name on the device. The player name can be entered manually or prefilled from Treasure Quest when that app is installed on the same Android device.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. After a successful purchase, Galaxy Knight may send a purchase log containing player name, app name, product id, amount, timestamp, week, and year to our purchase log endpoint for reconciliation. Purchase fulfillment does not depend on this log succeeding.

Advertising

Galaxy Knight does not contain ads and does not use advertising SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. We do not collect precise location, contacts, or device identifiers for tracking.

Children

The app avoids chat, social sharing, precise location, and gambling. Parents or guardians should review purchases and device-level parental controls.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
