Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vocab Quest ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Game Progress Data: Quiz scores, levels completed, achievements, coins earned, and learning statistics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality
  • Purchase Information: Transaction history for in-app purchases (processed securely through Google Play)
  • Settings Preferences: Language preferences, sound settings, and other app configurations

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Saving and restoring your game progress
  • Processing in-app purchases
  • Improving the app experience and fixing bugs
  • Personalizing content based on your preferences
  • Analyzing app usage to enhance features

4. Data Storage

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not store personal information on external servers unless required for specific features.

5. In-App Purchases

All in-app purchases are processed through Google Play. We do not have access to your payment card information. Google Play handles all payment processing securely according to their privacy policy.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases and app distribution
  • Google Fonts: For typography (no personal data collected)

7. Children's Privacy

Vocab Quest is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of optional data collection features
  • Request information about how your data is used

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
